Delivery information

We deliver carefully packed bouquets so they arrive fresh and photo-ready.

Yerevan

Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 14:00 on business days. Evening slots may be offered at checkout when capacity allows.

Across Armenia

Regional delivery typically takes 1–2 business days depending on location. You will see an estimated window before you pay.

Fees & free shipping

Delivery fees depend on distance and time slot. Orders over $35 qualify for free standard delivery within our published zones, matching the note on product pages.

Receiving your order

Please keep someone available at the address during your chosen window. If we cannot reach you, we will try once more the same day or reschedule according to our support policy.
